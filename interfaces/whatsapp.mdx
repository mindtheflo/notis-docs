# WhatsApp Integration

WhatsApp is one of the primary interfaces for interacting with Notis.ai. This guide explains how to set up and use Notis through WhatsApp, along with tips for getting the most out of this integration.

## Getting Started with Notis on WhatsApp

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add Notis to Your Contacts">
    Save Notis's WhatsApp number to your contacts:
    - Number: +1 (555) 123-4567
    - Name: Notis AI (or any name you prefer)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Start the Conversation">
    - Open WhatsApp and find Notis in your contacts
    - Send a message saying "Hello" or "Get Started"
    - Notis will respond with a welcome message and setup instructions
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Complete the Onboarding">
    - Follow the prompts to set your name and preferences
    - Connect your Notion account if you want to use that integration
    - Set up your default databases and preferences
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Using Voice Messages with Notis on WhatsApp

WhatsApp's voice message feature is the primary way to interact with Notis:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card
    title="Recording Voice Messages"
    icon="microphone"
  >
    - Tap and hold the microphone icon in WhatsApp
    - Speak your message clearly
    - Release to send the voice message to Notis
  </Card>
  
  <Card
    title="Voice Message Tips"
    icon="lightbulb"
  >
    - Keep messages under 2 minutes for best results
    - Start with a clear command (e.g., "Take a note about...")
    - Speak at a moderate pace with clear pronunciation
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## WhatsApp-Specific Features

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Quick Reply">
    Use WhatsApp's swipe-to-reply feature to respond directly to Notis's messages. This helps maintain context in conversations, especially when discussing specific notes or tasks.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Voice Note Transcription">
    All voice messages sent to Notis are automatically transcribed. You'll receive the transcription along with Notis's response, allowing you to verify the accuracy.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Media Sharing">
    Share images, documents, and other media with Notis through WhatsApp. Notis can process and store these attachments in your Notion workspace with appropriate context.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Location Sharing">
    Share your location with Notis to create location-based reminders or to add location context to your notes and tasks.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Tips for Using Notis with WhatsApp

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Use Status Updates">
    Check Notis's WhatsApp status for system updates, maintenance notifications, and new feature announcements.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Create Shortcuts">
    Use WhatsApp's chat shortcut feature to pin Notis to your home screen for quick access.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Batch Processing">
    You can send multiple voice messages in succession. Notis will process them in order and respond to each one.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Offline Usage">
    If you're offline, you can still record voice messages. They'll be sent to Notis when you reconnect, maintaining your workflow.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Privacy and Security

When using Notis with WhatsApp:

- All messages are end-to-end encrypted through WhatsApp's security
- Voice messages are processed securely and then deleted
- Your data is only accessible to you and not shared with third parties
- You can delete your data at any time through the privacy settings

## Troubleshooting WhatsApp Integration

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Notis Not Responding">
    - Check your internet connection
    - Verify that WhatsApp is up to date
    - Try sending a text message with "Ping" or "Hello"
    - If still unresponsive, try "Restart" to reset the conversation
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Voice Messages Not Transcribing Correctly">
    - Ensure you're in a quiet environment
    - Speak clearly and at a moderate pace
    - Try breaking longer messages into shorter segments
    - Use "Correct that to: [correct text]" to fix transcription errors
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Connection Issues">
    - Check if you can send regular WhatsApp messages to other contacts
    - Verify your internet connection is stable
    - Try switching between Wi-Fi and mobile data
    - Restart the WhatsApp application
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Notion Integration Problems">
    - Send "Check Notion connection" to verify the integration status
    - Try "Reconnect Notion" to reestablish the connection
    - Ensure your Notion workspace is accessible and permissions are correct
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
