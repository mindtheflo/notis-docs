# Email Integration

Email provides a versatile way to interact with Notis.ai, especially useful when you're working on a computer or don't have access to messaging apps. This guide explains how to set up and use Notis through email.

## Getting Started with Notis via Email

<Steps>
  <Step title="Send Your First Email">
    - Compose a new email to assistant@notis.ai
    - Subject: "Hello" or "Get Started"
    - Body can be empty or include a brief introduction
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Receive Setup Instructions">
    - Notis will reply with welcome information
    - Follow the instructions in the email to complete setup
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Complete the Onboarding">
    - Reply to the emails as instructed
    - Connect your Notion account if desired
    - Set your preferences for how Notis should process your emails
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Email-Specific Features

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Email Forwarding">
    Forward important emails to Notis to save information:
    
    - Forward confirmation emails to create related reminders
    - Forward project emails to extract tasks
    - Forward newsletters to save interesting articles
    
    Simply forward the email to assistant@notis.ai with an optional command in the subject line, such as "Save: Project Update" or "Extract Tasks"
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Email Templates">
    Create templates for recurring types of emails:
    
    - Weekly report template
    - Meeting notes template
    - Project update template
    
    Send an email with subject "Create Template: [Name]" and the template structure in the body. Then use it by sending an email with subject "Use Template: [Name]"
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Rich Text Formatting">
    Email supports rich text formatting that Notis can process:
    
    - Bold and italic text for emphasis
    - Bullet points and numbered lists
    - Headings and subheadings
    - Links to websites or resources
    
    Notis will preserve this formatting when saving your notes to Notion.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Scheduled Emails">
    Set up scheduled emails to Notis for recurring tasks:
    
    - Daily morning email with today's focus
    - Weekly review email on Friday afternoons
    - Monthly planning email at the start of each month
    
    Use your email client's scheduling feature to send these automatically.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Tips for Using Notis with Email

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Use Clear Subject Lines">
    Make your subject lines specific and action-oriented. This helps Notis understand your intent and process the email correctly.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Create Email Filters">
    Set up filters in your email client to organize responses from Notis into specific folders, keeping your inbox clean.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Use Email Signatures">
    Create different email signatures for different types of requests to Notis, with pre-formatted commands or templates.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Batch Processing">
    Save up several related items and send them in a single email with clear section breaks, rather than sending multiple separate emails.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Privacy and Security

When using Notis with email:

- All emails are processed securely on encrypted servers
- Attachments are scanned for viruses before processing
- Your data is only accessible to you and not shared with third parties
- You can request deletion of your data at any time

## Troubleshooting Email Integration

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Notis Not Responding">
    - Check if the email was sent to the correct address (assistant@notis.ai)
    - Check your spam folder for responses
    - Verify that your email account is not blocking Notis's responses
    - Try sending a simple email with subject "Ping" to test the connection
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Commands Not Recognized">
    - Make sure commands are in the subject line, not just the body
    - Check for typos in command syntax
    - Use simple, clear commands (e.g., "Note:" instead of "Please create a note about:")
    - Try using a different command format if one isn't working
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Attachments Not Processing">
    - Check that the attachment is in a supported format
    - Verify that the file size is under the limit (typically 25MB)
    - Make sure the attachment was actually included (check sent items)
    - Try sending the attachment in a different format if possible
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Notion Integration Problems">
    - Send an email with subject "Check Notion Connection" to verify status
    - Follow the instructions to reconnect if necessary
    - Ensure your Notion workspace is accessible and permissions are correct
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
