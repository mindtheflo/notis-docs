# Setting Up Notis.ai

This guide provides detailed instructions for setting up Notis.ai with your preferred messaging platform and configuring it to work with your Notion workspace.

## Prerequisites

Before you begin, make sure you have:

- A smartphone with WhatsApp, Telegram, or an email account
- A Notion account (free or paid)
- Internet connection

## Detailed Setup Process

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="WhatsApp">
    ### Setting up Notis on WhatsApp
    
    1. **Add Notis to your contacts**
       - Save Notis's phone number: +1 (555) 123-4567
       - Open WhatsApp and find the contact
    
    2. **Start the conversation**
       - Send "Hello" to begin
       - Notis will respond with a welcome message and setup instructions
    
    3. **Complete the onboarding**
       - Follow the prompts to set your name and preferences
       - Notis will guide you through connecting to Notion
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Telegram">
    ### Setting up Notis on Telegram
    
    1. **Find the Notis bot**
       - Open Telegram and search for "@NotisAIBot"
       - Tap on the bot in search results
    
    2. **Start the conversation**
       - Tap the "Start" button or send "/start"
       - Notis will introduce itself and provide setup instructions
    
    3. **Complete the onboarding**
       - Follow the prompts to set your name and preferences
       - Notis will guide you through connecting to Notion
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Email">
    ### Setting up Notis via Email
    
    1. **Send your first email**
       - Compose a new email to assistant@notis.ai
       - Subject: "Hello" or "Get Started"
       - Body can be empty
    
    2. **Receive setup instructions**
       - Notis will reply with welcome information
       - Follow the instructions in the email
    
    3. **Complete the onboarding**
       - Reply to the emails as instructed
       - Notis will guide you through connecting to Notion
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Connecting to Notion

<Steps>
  <Step title="Initiate Notion Connection">
    Send the message "Connect Notion" to Notis through your chosen platform
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Authorize Access">
    1. Click the link Notis sends you
    2. Sign in to your Notion account if prompted
    3. Review the permissions Notis is requesting
    4. Click "Authorize"
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select Databases">
    1. Notis will ask which databases you want to use
    2. Reply with the names or select from options provided
    3. Confirm your selections
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Verify Connection">
    1. Notis will confirm the connection is successful
    2. Try a test command like "Create a new task: Test Notion integration"
    3. Check your Notion workspace to see the new entry
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Customizing Your Setup

After the basic setup, you can customize Notis to better fit your workflow:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Setting Default Databases">
    You can set default databases for different types of content:
    
    - Send "Set default tasks database to [Database Name]"
    - Send "Set default notes database to [Database Name]"
    - Send "Set default calendar to [Database Name]"
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Configuring Notification Preferences">
    Control how and when Notis sends you notifications:
    
    - Send "Notification settings" to see current settings
    - Reply with options like "Reminders only", "All messages", or "Silent mode"
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Setting Up Templates">
    Create templates for recurring types of notes:
    
    - Send "Create template" to start the process
    - Follow the prompts to name and define your template
    - Use templates by saying "Use template [name]" before your message
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Troubleshooting

If you encounter issues during setup:

- **Connection Problems**: Make sure you have a stable internet connection
- **Notion Authorization Fails**: Try clearing your browser cache and cookies, then try again
- **Messages Not Being Processed**: Check if you've reached your daily limit (free tier) or contact support
- **Can't Find Databases**: Make sure you've shared the databases with Notis during authorization

For additional help, contact support@notis.ai or send "Help" to Notis through your messaging platform.
