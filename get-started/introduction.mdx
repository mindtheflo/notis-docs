## Why Notis.ai?

Notis.ai transforms how you capture and organize information by:

- **Turning voice into text** - Speak naturally and Notis transcribes your thoughts
- **Organizing automatically** - Your notes are categorized and stored in your personal knowledge system
- **Accessible everywhere** - Use Notis through WhatsApp, Telegram, Email, and more
- **Syncing with Notion** - All your notes are seamlessly integrated with your Notion workspace
