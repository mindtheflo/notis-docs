# Quick Start Guide

This guide will help you set up Notis.ai and start using it right away. Follow these simple steps to begin capturing and organizing your thoughts with your voice.

## Step 2: Connect with Notis

Depending on your chosen platform:

- **WhatsApp**: Add Notis's number to your contacts and send a message to start
- **Telegram**: Search for the Notis bot (@NotisAIBot) and start a conversation
- **Email**: Send an email to assistant@notis.ai with "Hello" in the subject line

## Step 3: Set Up Notion Integration

To get the most out of Notis, connect it to your Notion workspace:

1. Send the message "Connect Notion" to Notis
2. Click the link Notis sends you
3. Authorize Notis to access your Notion workspace
4. Select which Notion databases you want Notis to use

## Step 4: Try Your First Voice Note

Now you're ready to create your first voice note:

1. Open your conversation with Notis
2. Record a voice message (e.g., "Remind me to buy groceries tomorrow at 5 PM")
3. Send it to Notis
4. Notis will transcribe your message, create a reminder, and confirm
